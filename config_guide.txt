4.14 Practical tuning guidance (how to change settings safely)
If the agent never reaches the target: Increase PROGRESS_REWARD , reduce STEP_PENALTY mag-
nitude, increase MAX_ACTIONS_PER_EPISODE , or expand AMP_BINS /OFFSET_BINS .
If the agent uses overly strong hills: Increase BIAS_PENALTY , shrink AMP_BINS , and/or set a
realistic IN_ZONE_MAX_AMP with masking.
If dynamics are too noisy: Lower TEMPERATURE , increase FRICTION , or lower DT.
If PPO becomes unstable (NaNs, oscillation): Lower LR, increase BATCH_SIZE , reduce ENT_COEF ,
and/or reduce CLIP_RANGE . Consider lowering PPO_TARGET_KL to stop updates earlier.
